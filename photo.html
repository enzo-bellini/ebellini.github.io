Photo for my photography project

The photography project required me to capture and edit images and use multiple composition methoids including symetry, point of view, rule of thirds, leading lines, and framing. These photos needed to be taken in natural and artificial light. Eight total photos needed to be taken and four were edited. Editing techniques used included cropping, tonal value, contrast, brightness, and file format.
