The video project was one of the harder projects done in AENG 110. This project required taking and editing a sixty to seventy-five minute video. Our first step was to create a script for the video. Our next step was to create a plan of action and see our steps of producing the video. My group did our video on the "trick or treat trail" heald by campus life. We explained the event and interviewed some trick or treaters and staff to make the video interesting and informational. part of our purpose in this video was to inform the next generation of freshmen about what we do here on campus. My favorite part was recording the walkup to margaritaville.
